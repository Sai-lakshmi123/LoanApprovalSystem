🔧 6 TOOLS AVAILABLE
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Tool 1: evaluate_dti_ratio
  Purpose: Calculate debt-to-income ratio with risk level
  Returns: DTI %, risk level, category, eligibility flags

Tool 2: evaluate_credit_risk
  Purpose: Assess credit score risk with adjustments
  Adjustments: Delinquencies, recent inquiries
  Returns: Score, category, adjusted risk level

Tool 3: evaluate_loan_amount_risk
  Purpose: Evaluate loan size relative to income/property
  Metrics: LTI (Loan-to-Income), LTV (Loan-to-Value)
  Returns: Risk levels and debt burden analysis

Tool 4: detect_risk_anomalies
  Purpose: Identify unusual patterns and red flags
  Anomaly Types: 9 different types detected
  Severity Levels: Critical, High, Medium, Low

Tool 5: generate_risk_report ⭐ (Most Useful)
  Purpose: Comprehensive financial risk assessment
  Includes: All DTI, credit, loan, anomaly data
  Returns: Risk level, score, recommendation, reasoning

Tool 6: evaluate_with_scenario_analysis
  Purpose: Test multiple loan amounts
  Scenarios: Conservative, moderate, aggressive
  Returns: DTI and risk for each scenario

💡 USAGE EXAMPLE
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

from agents.financial_risk_analysis_agent import FinancialRiskAnalysisAgent

agent = FinancialRiskAnalysisAgent()

result = agent.analyze_financial_risk(
    applicant_id="APP001",
    applicant_data={
        "annual_income": 150000,
        "existing_monthly_debt": 1000,
        "credit_score": 760,
        "delinquencies": 0,
        "inquiries_last_6_months": 1,
        "credit_utilization": 0.30,
        "years_at_current_job": 8,
        "existing_loans": 1
    },
    loan_request={
        "loan_amount": 300000,
        "property_value": 500000,
        "loan_term_months": 360
    }
)

if result["status"] == "success":
    analysis = result["analysis"]
    print(f"DTI: {analysis['dti_analysis']['dti_with_new_loan_percentage']}%")
    print(f"Credit Risk: {analysis['credit_risk']['adjusted_risk_level']}")
    print(f"Risk Score: {analysis['aggregate_risk_assessment']['overall_risk_score']}/5")
    print(f"Recommendation: {analysis['aggregate_risk_assessment']['recommendation']}")


📈 RISK ASSESSMENT METRICS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

DTI RATIO (Debt-to-Income):
  < 20%:   Very Low Risk ✅
  20-36%:  Low Risk ✅
  36-43%:  Medium Risk ⚠️
  43-50%:  High Risk ❌
  > 50%:   Very High Risk ❌❌

CREDIT SCORE RISK:
  750+:    Excellent (Very Low Risk) ✅
  700-749: Good (Low Risk) ✅
  650-699: Fair (Medium Risk) ⚠️
  600-649: Poor (High Risk) ❌
  < 600:   Very Poor (Very High Risk) ❌❌

LOAN-TO-INCOME (LTI):
  < 3%:   Very Low Risk ✅
  3-5%:   Low Risk ✅
  5-8%:   Medium Risk ⚠️
  8-12%:  High Risk ❌
  > 12%:  Very High Risk ❌❌

LOAN-TO-VALUE (LTV):
  < 60%:   Excellent ✅
  60-80%:  Good ✅
  80-95%:  Acceptable ⚠️
  > 95%:   High Risk ❌

AGGREGATE RISK SCORE:
  0-1.5:   Very Low (APPROVE) ✅
  1.5-2.5: Low (CONDITIONAL) ✅
  2.5-3.5: Medium (REVIEW) ⚠️
  3.5-4.5: High (UNDERWRITING) ❌
  4.5-5:   Very High (DECLINE) ❌❌

🚨 TROUBLESHOOTING
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Issue: Connection Refused
  Cause: RiskRulesDB server not running
  Solution: python mcp/riskrulesdb/server.py

Issue: Invalid API Key
  Cause: ANTHROPIC_API_KEY not set
  Solution: export ANTHROPIC_API_KEY=sk-...

Issue: JSON Parse Error
  Cause: Claude output format invalid
  Solution: Check response, may need prompt adjustment

Issue: Tool Not Found
  Cause: RiskRulesDB missing tool
  Solution: Verify all 6 tools available in RiskRulesDB

Issue: Timeout
  Cause: Slow database or network
  Solution: Increase timeout: RiskRulesDBClient(timeout=60)

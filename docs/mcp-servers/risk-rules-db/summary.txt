🛠️ 6 RISK EVALUATION TOOLS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Tool 1: evaluate_dti_ratio
├─ Purpose: Calculate Debt-to-Income ratio
├─ Returns: DTI %, risk level, category, eligibility flags
├─ Thresholds: < 20% (Very Low) to > 50% (Very High)
└─ Example: client.evaluate_dti_ratio(5000, 1500)

Tool 2: evaluate_credit_risk
├─ Purpose: Assess credit score risk (adjusted)
├─ Adjustments: Delinquencies, recent inquiries
├─ Categories: Excellent (750+) to Very Poor (< 600)
└─ Example: client.evaluate_credit_risk(750, 0, 1)

Tool 3: evaluate_loan_amount_risk
├─ Purpose: Evaluate loan size relative to income/property
├─ Metrics: LTI (Loan-to-Income), LTV (Loan-to-Value)
├─ Risk Level: Very Low to Very High
└─ Example: client.evaluate_loan_amount_risk(300000, 100000, ...)

Tool 4: detect_risk_anomalies
├─ Purpose: Identify unusual patterns and red flags
├─ Anomaly Types: 9 different types detected
├─ Severity Levels: Critical, High, Medium, Low
└─ Example: client.detect_risk_anomalies(applicant, loan)

Tool 5: generate_risk_report ⭐ (Most Useful)
├─ Purpose: Comprehensive financial risk assessment
├─ Includes: All DTI, credit, loan, anomaly data
├─ Output: Risk level, score, recommendation, reasoning
└─ Example: client.generate_risk_report("APP001", applicant, loan)

Tool 6: evaluate_with_scenario_analysis
├─ Purpose: Test multiple loan amounts
├─ Scenarios: Conservative, moderate, aggressive
├─ Output: DTI and risk for each scenario
└─ Example: client.evaluate_with_scenario_analysis(..., scenarios)

📊 KEY METRICS & THRESHOLDS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

DTI RATIO:
  < 20%:     Very Low Risk (Excellent) ✅
  20-36%:    Low Risk (Good) ✅
  36-43%:    Medium Risk (Acceptable) ⚠️
  43-50%:    High Risk ❌
  > 50%:     Very High Risk ❌❌

CREDIT SCORE:
  750-850:   Excellent (Very Low Risk) ✅
  700-749:   Good (Low Risk) ✅
  650-699:   Fair (Medium Risk) ⚠️
  600-649:   Poor (High Risk) ❌
  < 600:     Very Poor (Very High Risk) ❌❌

LOAN-TO-INCOME:
  < 3%:      Very Low Risk ✅
  3-5%:      Low Risk ✅
  5-8%:      Medium Risk ⚠️
  8-12%:     High Risk ❌
  > 12%:     Very High Risk ❌❌

LOAN-TO-VALUE:
  < 60%:     Excellent ✅
  60-80%:    Good ✅
  80-95%:    Acceptable ⚠️
  > 95%:     High Risk ❌

📈 SCORING FORMULAS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

DTI:
  DTI Ratio = Monthly Debt / Monthly Income
  DTI % = DTI Ratio * 100

Credit Risk:
  Base Score = Determined by credit score (5-85)
  Delinquency Penalty = Delinquencies * 15
  Inquiry Penalty = min(Inquiries * 3, 25)
  Adjusted Score = Base + Delinquency + Inquiry

Loan Amount:
  LTI % = (Loan Amount / Annual Income) * 100
  LTV % = (Loan Amount / Property Value) * 100
  Overall = Average of LTI Risk, LTV Risk, Debt Burden Risk

Anomaly Score:
  Each anomaly scored 30-100
  Overall = Average of all anomaly scores

🆘 TROUBLESHOOTING

Tool Not Found:
  Check tool name and parameters match documentation

Timeout:
  Increase client timeout: client.timeout = 60

Port Already in Use:
  Change port in server.py or kill existing process: lsof -i :3001
